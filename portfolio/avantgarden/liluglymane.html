lil ugly mane

Riding the wave of his newly released album ‘Mista Thug Isolation’ , Lil Ugly Mane will be washing up on the shores of Aotearoa to drop his take on Chopped N Screwed Rap music. Who can say what the performance will be like? It will be his first time performing on a stage as opposed to a cave and the acoustics are heaps different. We don’t think there will be any ouija boards or seances during ‘Playaz Circle’, ( L U M’s number one hit of 2011 )but we can’t be certain there won’t. For any sudden possessions ( we mean of the biblical kind) during his set we have a certified exorcist on site to keep everyone with that PMA! Don’t miss this artist, who knows when he will leave the cave again hahaha
